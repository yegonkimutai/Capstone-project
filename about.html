BRILLIANT CODERS INTERNATIONAL SHOW 2023


The International Show brings together the community of experts, academics, and activists who comprise the Brilliant Coders affiliate network in a different country every five years. This year, we hope to expand our invitation list including organizations and individuals who want to work with us on shared projects that advance the cause of the Coders, free culture and open knowledge. So if you're active and engaged in the worlds of open content and knowledge - free software advocates, wikipedians, Open knowledge, galleries, libraries, museums, archives, governments and foundations, lawyers, and activists - we hope you'll consider joining us this year to build a stronger, more vibrant community together


Please contact us per Email for any further questions about the showcase. bcintshow2023@cckenya.org
